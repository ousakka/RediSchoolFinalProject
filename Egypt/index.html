Egypt

Egypt is a land where ancient history meets vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes. Walk among the timeless pyramids and temples, sail the legendary Nile River, and relax on the sun-kissed beaches of the Red Sea. From bustling markets to serene desert sunsets, Egypt offers unforgettable experiences that captivate every traveler’s heart.







Must-see in summer




  • Hurghada

    Hurghada is a Red Sea paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and endless sunshine. It’s perfect for snorkeling, diving, and relaxing on sandy beaches. With lively resorts, desert excursions, and a laid-back vibe, Hurghada is a top summer escape in Egypt.

  • Sharm El Sheikh

    Sharm El Sheikh is a stunning coastal city where desert meets the deep blue sea. Famed for its world-class diving spots, luxurious resorts, and golden beaches, it's a favorite for both adventure and relaxation. Whether you're exploring coral reefs or watching the sunset over the Sinai mountains, Sharm offers a summer escape you won’t forget.

  • Alexandria

    Alexandria blends Mediterranean charm with layers of history. Walk along the breezy Corniche, visit ancient ruins and the modern Library of Alexandria, and enjoy fresh seafood by the sea. With a cooler coastal climate, it’s a perfect summer retreat rich in culture and coastal beauty.







Must-see in winter




  • Giza

    Giza is home to Egypt’s most iconic wonders:the Pyramids and the Sphinx. Standing in their shadow, you’ll feel the awe of ancient history come alive. Just outside the energy of Cairo, Giza offers an unforgettable journey into the heart of one of the world’s oldest civilizations.

  • Luxor

    Luxor is an open-air museum where ancient Egypt comes to life. Explore the majestic temples of Karnak and Luxor, wander through the Valley of the Kings, and sail the Nile at sunset. With every step, you walk through history, surrounded by timeless beauty and mystery.

  • Aswan

    Aswan is a peaceful Nile-side city filled with rich history and natural beauty. Visit ancient temples, sail on a traditional felucca, and relax by the river surrounded by palm trees and golden sands. It’s a place where time slows down and the charm of southern Egypt shines through.
